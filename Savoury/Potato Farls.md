# Potato Farls

## Ingredients
* 250-300g Mashed Potato, with Butter and perhaps Soured Cream, seasoned

* 50-60g Plain Flour

## Method

1. The potato should be warm, either freshly mashed, 
or warm it in the microwave for 30 seconds.

1. Add the potato and flour to a mixing bowl, mash it together 
with a fork or a mixing machine until it just comes together as a dough.

1. Shape the dough into a flattened ball, wrap in clingfilm and chill.

1. Roll out the dough on a sheet of parchment util about 5mm thick.

1. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes at 180Â°, turning over halfway through.

1. Cut into slices and serve hot  
*OR*  
Allow to cool and slice for later. It can be reheated in the oven,
a dry frying pan, or even a toaster.
